Kanyakumari is popular because it is the only places on earth, where you can see the Sun Rise and Sun Set from the ocean. It is the only place in India where one can enjoy the unique spectacle of Sunset and Moonrise simultaneously on full moon days.



Elliot's Beach, Chennai One of the cleanest beaches in Chennai is Elliot's Beach situated in Besant Nagar, one of the hippest neighborhoods in the city. Enveloped in a friendly vibe, it is a must-visit beach, especially in the evenings when youngsters and families throng the beach.
